# Rapture Surfcamps vs Kima Surf — Comprehensive Comparison

## TL;DR

Rapture and Kima are both established surf camp operators competing primarily in Bali, with overlapping locations and a similar German-speaking target audience. Rapture (est. 2003) offers a broader geographic spread across Bali, Portugal, Costa Rica, Morocco, and Nicaragua, while Kima (surfaris since 1995, camps since ~2010s) is deeply specialized in Bali and Sri Lanka with 6 camps across just 2 countries. Kima offers the most extensive surf guiding program of any competitor (2x daily, 7 days/week, included in price), while Rapture differentiates with more destination variety, premium villa options, and a stronger food program.

## Detailed Comparison

### 1. Destinations & Specialization

**Rapture** spans multiple continents:
- **Bali** — Green Bowl + Padang Padang (both in the coveted Bukit peninsula)
- **Portugal** — Ericeira, Coxos Surf Villa, Milfontes (3 distinct camps)
- **Costa Rica** — Avellanas (Guanacaste)
- **Morocco** — Banana Village
- **Nicaragua** — Maderas

**Kima** goes deep on 2 destinations:
- **Bali** — 4 camps: Canggu (hip, social), Seminyak (tropical oasis), Uluwatu (world-class breaks), Bukit (luxury VIP, from €2,100/week all-inclusive)
- **Sri Lanka** — 2 camps: Weligama (consistent beginner-friendly waves) + Hiriketiya (jungle bungalows)

**Verdict**: Kima is the Bali specialist with unmatched local depth (4 different Bali locations covering every vibe from budget dorms to luxury). Rapture offers diversity across continents, ideal for travelers who want to surf different parts of the world. If you only want Bali/Sri Lanka, Kima has more options. If you want Portugal, Costa Rica, or Morocco, Rapture is the only choice.

---

### 2. Accommodation

**Rapture**:
- Shared rooms (4–6 beds), twin/double, private rooms, glamping tents
- Premium options: Coxos Surf Villa (infinity pool), Green Bowl (beachfront)
- Linen, towels, daily cleaning, AC on indoor rooms

**Kima**:
- Massive range from budget to luxury:
  - **Dorms** — from €20/night (Seminyak) to €24/night (Canggu)
  - **Standard/Superior rooms** — mid-range private options
  - **Deluxe suites** — premium with full amenities
  - **Private villas** — 2-bedroom villas in Canggu; full villa experience at Bukit
  - **Beachfront cabanas** — poolside/plunge pool options at Weligama
- Properties feature: pools, gyms (fully equipped), beach volleyball, 15m climbing walls, 5m deep diving pools, counter-current swimming, spa/massage rooms, co-working spaces

**Verdict**: Kima has significantly more accommodation tiers and resort-grade amenities (climbing walls, diving pools, ice baths are genuinely unique). Rapture offers a tighter but well-curated range with standout premium options like Coxos villa.

---

### 3. Surf Coaching & Guiding

**Rapture**:
- Lessons grouped by skill level
- ~2 sessions/day (morning 2–3h, afternoon 1.5–2h)
- Max 5:1 ratio
- All equipment included
- Surf theory + weekly video analysis included
- Surf guiding available as add-on

**Kima**:
- **Surf guiding included** in every booking — 2x daily, 7 days a week
- Groups max 6 surfers, divided by skill level
- Guides organize up to 16 trips per day across all levels
- Surf theory 6x/week + filmed sessions/video analysis 6x/week (all included)
- Separate 5-day Learn-to-Surf package for absolute beginners (paid add-on)
- Professional, certified surf guides in water with you at all times
- Boards provided but rental is a separate fee

**Verdict**: Kima's surf guiding program is industry-leading — 14 guided sessions per week, included in the room price, is hard to beat. However, Rapture's structured coaching with a 5:1 ratio may be better for beginners who need hands-on teaching rather than guiding. Kima separates "lessons" (beginners) from "guiding" (everyone else), which means beginners pay extra for the Learn-to-Surf course. At Rapture, lessons for all levels are included.

---

### 4. Food & Dining

**Rapture**:
- Breakfast + dinner Mon–Fri included
- 3-course breakfasts and dinners
- Vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free options
- Rotating menus

**Kima**:
- 3-course breakfast included (7am–12pm)
- Dinner NOT included at most Bali camps (Weligama includes dinner on weeknights)
- On-site restaurants with international, Indonesian, vegan/vegetarian options (7am–11pm)
- Free tea/coffee until 6pm
- Complimentary BBQ once a week
- Cost of eating out in Bali noted as "around €3 for a filling meal"

**Verdict**: Rapture includes significantly more food value with breakfast AND dinner. At Kima, you'll eat well from the included breakfast and cheap local food, but dinner costs add up over a week. For travelers who want everything pre-planned, Rapture wins. For those who want to explore Bali's food scene independently, Kima's approach works.

---

### 5. Pricing

**Bali comparison (1 week):**

| Room Type | Rapture | Kima |
|---|---|---|
| **Dorm/Shared** | ~€500 (all-in with meals + lessons) | ~€168 (Canggu dorm, breakfast + guiding only) |
| **Private room** | €600–800 est. | From €252/week (Uluwatu) to €350+ (Canggu superior) |
| **Premium/Villa** | Coxos villa pricing (premium) | €2,100/week (Bukit VIP, full board + chauffeur) |

**Important caveat**: Kima's base prices are accommodation + breakfast + surf guiding. No dinner, no lessons (beginners need Learn-to-Surf at extra cost), no board rental included. Rapture's prices include meals + lessons + equipment. When you factor in dinner (~€21/week cheap), board rental, and surf school for beginners, Kima's total cost approaches Rapture's.

**Kima discounts**: 1% discount per night stayed (e.g., 14 nights = 14% off). Loyalty program for returning guests.

**Verdict**: Kima appears cheaper at face value, especially for experienced surfers who don't need lessons and want to eat out. Rapture offers better value for beginners and those who want an all-inclusive price. Kima's Bukit VIP package (€2,100/week with full board, chauffeur, beach club) has no Rapture equivalent.

### 6. Facilities & Extras

**Rapture**:
- Pools (Coxos infinity pool notable)
- Outdoor cinema (Costa Rica)
- Recovery centre (Costa Rica)
- Gym (Costa Rica)
- Bikes (Portugal)
- Live surf forecast widgets on camp pages
- Retreat hosting program

**Kima**:
- Multiple pools (including 5m deep diving pool with counter-current)
- Fully equipped functional gyms
- 15m natural climbing walls
- Beach volleyball courts
- Ice baths (Bali + Sri Lanka)
- Spa/Ayurvedic massage rooms
- Co-working spaces with high-speed internet
- Surfskate bowls
- HIIT and martial arts workouts
- Free bikes and camp vans

**Verdict**: Kima's facilities are resort-grade and genuinely impressive. Climbing walls, ice baths, deep diving pools, and counter-current systems are unique in the surf camp world. Rapture offers a more focused set of amenities but with unique touches like retreat hosting and outdoor cinema.

---

### 7. Target Audience

**Rapture**: Beginner through advanced surfers, solo travelers (~60% at some locations), digital nomads, couples, retreat groups. Strong German-speaking market. More even age distribution.

**Kima**: Primarily surfers with some experience (guiding model rewards those who can already surf). Strong German market (German website, German-origin brand). Attracts fitness-oriented travelers (gym, HIIT, climbing, martial arts, ice baths). Digital nomads (co-working spaces). Couples and solo travelers.

---

## Who Should Choose Rapture Over Kima?

- **Complete beginners** who want lessons included in the price
- **Travelers who want multiple continents** — Portugal, Costa Rica, Morocco
- **Those who prefer all-inclusive pricing** (meals + lessons + equipment)
- **Premium villa seekers** in Portugal (Coxos Surf Villa)
- **Retreat organizers** looking for a hosting partner
- **Surfers who value instructor-led coaching** over self-guided sessions

## Who Should Choose Kima Over Rapture?

- **Experienced surfers** who want maximum water time (14 guided sessions/week included)
- **Bali specialists** who want to choose between 4 distinct Bali locations
- **Sri Lanka travelers** — Kima has 2 dedicated camps; Rapture has none
- **Fitness enthusiasts** who want climbing walls, gyms, ice baths, martial arts
- **Budget-conscious surfers** who can skip lessons and eat locally
- **Long-stay travelers** — Kima's 1%-per-night discount rewards extended stays
- **Digital nomads** seeking dedicated co-working spaces
- **Luxury seekers** — Kima Bukit's VIP package (€2,100/week all-inclusive with chauffeur)
