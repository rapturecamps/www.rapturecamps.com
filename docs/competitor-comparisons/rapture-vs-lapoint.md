# Rapture Surfcamps vs Lapoint Surf Camps — Comprehensive Comparison

## TL;DR

Rapture (est. 2003) and Lapoint (est. 2008) are both established multi-destination surf camp operators with overlapping locations in Bali, Portugal, Costa Rica, and Sri Lanka. Lapoint is the larger, more polished operation with a Scandinavian brand identity, fixed arrival dates, and a structured level-based surf course system (Levels 1–4). Rapture is the more experienced operator with a flexible arrival approach, all-inclusive pricing, stronger food program, and intimate small-camp feel. Lapoint excels at community building and marketing; Rapture excels at surf coaching depth and value.

## Detailed Comparison

### 1. Destinations & Reach

**Rapture** operates in:
- **Bali** — Green Bowl + Padang Padang (Bukit peninsula)
- **Portugal** — Ericeira (World Surfing Reserve), Coxos Surf Villa, Milfontes (Alentejo)
- **Costa Rica** — Avellanas
- **Morocco** — Banana Village
- **Nicaragua** — Maderas

**Lapoint** operates in:
- **Portugal** — Ericeira + Alentejo (direct overlap with Rapture)
- **Bali** — Canggu (different location than Rapture's Bukit camps)
- **Sri Lanka** — Ahangama
- **Costa Rica** — Santa Teresa (different from Rapture's Avellanas)
- **Nicaragua** — Los Clavos (premium camp)
- **El Salvador** — El Sunzal (premium camp)
- **Spain** — Fuerteventura
- **Norway** — Hoddevik (partner camp)
- **Maldives** — Surf cruise

**Verdict**: Lapoint has more destinations (10 vs. 6–8) and exclusive offerings (Maldives surf cruise, Norway, El Salvador). Rapture has Morocco and direct Bukit/Padang Padang access in Bali (Lapoint is in Canggu, different vibe and waves). Both compete head-to-head in Portugal and Central America.

---

### 2. Accommodation

**Rapture**:
- Shared rooms (4–6 beds), twin/double, private rooms, glamping tents
- Premium: Coxos Surf Villa (infinity pool), Green Bowl (beachfront)
- Linen, towels, daily cleaning, AC on indoor rooms
- Range from social dorms to luxury villa experiences

**Lapoint**:
- Dorms, twin rooms, double rooms
- Traditional Balinese garden-style compound in Bali with pools, volleyball, sunbeds
- Modern facilities combined with local architectural style
- Pool, training space, social areas, yoga space at all camps
- No explicit glamping or premium villa tier mentioned

**Verdict**: Rapture offers more accommodation variety and a higher premium tier (Coxos villa, Green Bowl beachfront). Lapoint's properties are well-designed and social but more standardized across locations. Rapture has more options for couples or those wanting privacy/luxury.

---

### 3. Surf Coaching

**Rapture**:
- Lessons grouped by skill level
- ~2 sessions/day (morning + afternoon)
- Max 5:1 student-to-instructor ratio
- All equipment included
- Surf theory + weekly video analysis included in base price
- Surf guiding available as add-on

**Lapoint**:
- Structured 4-level system: Level 1 (beginner) → Level 4 (advanced) + Surf Guiding
- 15+ years of surf course experience
- "Right Background, Right Feedback, Right Waves" methodology
- Small groups that get smaller at higher levels
- Local coaches who scout conditions daily
- Flexibility to change level during the week
- Multiple package types: standard, intensive (2x daily), guiding (advanced), 1:1 coaching
- Longboarding-specific course in Bali (5-day program with full quiver)
- Level-based sub-grouping for more personalized feedback

**Verdict**: Lapoint has a more formalized and marketed coaching framework with clever packaging (intensive, 1:1, longboarding). Rapture's coaching is comprehensive but less productized. Rapture's 5:1 ratio is competitive. Lapoint's variety of packages (especially 1:1 coaching and longboarding) gives advanced surfers more options. Both include equipment; both have strong instructor teams.

---

### 4. Food & Dining

**Rapture**:
- Breakfast + dinner Mon–Fri included in all packages
- 3-course breakfasts (full spread with smoothie bowls, eggs, fruit, unlimited coffee)
- 3-course dinners with rotating menus
- Vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free options

**Lapoint**:
- Breakfast included (fresh fruit, granola, cereals, toast, eggs, pancakes)
- Dinner varies by camp — some included, some not
- "Home made by our lovely staff" at Bali
- Focus on local food culture

**Verdict**: Rapture includes more meals consistently across all destinations. Lapoint's meal inclusion varies and dinner is not always guaranteed. For travelers who want predictable, included dining, Rapture is the better choice.

### 6. Community & Brand

**Rapture**:
- "All you have is now" — action-oriented, present-moment messaging
- Australian photographer + Austrian entrepreneur origin story
- 85k+ past guests, 23 TripAdvisor awards
- More understated, substance-over-style marketing
- German + English markets
- Retreat hosting for groups

**Lapoint**:
- "Life should feel like this every day" — aspirational lifestyle brand
- Scandinavian quality + global crew positioning
- 100k+ guests
- Heavy content marketing (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram)
- Celebrity/influencer partnerships (pro surfer Laura Crane, Olympic freeskier Birk Ruud)
- Fixed arrival dates for community cohesion (everyone starts together)
- 40% solo travelers
- Membership community with €30 off next trip
- 14-day free rebooking guarantee
- Code of Conduct + Sexual Harassment Policy publicly shared
- 8-language website (EN, SE, NO, DK, DE, CH, AT, FR)

**Verdict**: Lapoint is the more polished marketing operation with stronger brand consistency, wider language reach, and better content marketing. Rapture has deeper heritage (2003 vs. 2008) and more authentic "built by surfers" credibility. Lapoint's fixed arrival days create tighter weekly cohorts; Rapture's flexibility is better for travelers with variable schedules.

## Who Should Choose Rapture Over Lapoint?

- **Surfers who want all-inclusive pricing** with meals + coaching included
- **Bali Bukit/Padang Padang seekers** — Rapture has direct access to world-class reef breaks vs. Lapoint's Canggu beach breaks
- **Morocco travelers** — Rapture has a camp there; Lapoint doesn't
- **Those who prefer flexible arrival dates** rather than fixed weekly cohorts
- **Couples/premium seekers** wanting villa-level accommodation (Coxos)
- **Retreat organizers** looking for a hosting partner
- **Travelers who prioritize food quality** and included dinners

## Who Should Choose Lapoint Over Rapture?

- **Solo travelers** seeking a tight-knit weekly community (fixed arrival dates)
- **Budget Bali travelers** — Lapoint starts at €396/week (4-day options available)
- **Advanced surfers** wanting 1:1 coaching or longboarding-specific programs
- **Digital nomads** — Work & Surf package with coworking in Ericeira
- **Scandinavian travelers** — 4 Nordic language sites, Scandi brand identity
- **Bucket-list surfers** — Maldives cruise, Norway arctic surfing
- **El Salvador / Fuerteventura** seekers — destinations Rapture doesn't cover
- **Content-driven travelers** who research via YouTube/TikTok and want that polished brand experience
